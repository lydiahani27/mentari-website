Our History

Four bookworms who met in diploma reunited at Lydia's wedding at age 25, where we discovered our shared "problem" - too many unread books collecting dust! Between wedding toasts and laughs, we hatched a plan to turn our book hoarding into a rental service, proving that sometimes the best ideas come from old friends and too many books.

MISSION
We transform private book collections into accessible treasures, creating a sustainable reading ecosystem that brings stories to life through sharing, connecting readers across our community while giving pre-loved books new purpose.
VISION
To create a community where every book finds its reader and every reader discovers their next adventure, transforming personal libraries into bridges that connect people through stories.
