How We Value??

All books we receive are processed by an in-house algorithm, which takes into account the existing firsthand prices, condition, recency, popularity,and ratings of the books.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

The total trade-in value is calculated based on an in-house algorithm that takes into consideration factors like:

  • the condition of the books
  • the firsthand price of the books
  • the recency of the books
  • the popularity of the books
  • ratings of the books

Books that fail to meet our acceptance guidelines will be recycled or donated responsibly. Kindly note that Mentari does not process any return of any bookss once they have been sent in.

You will receive your Credit Points in the form of a digital gift card in 3-5 days from the date of your drop-off/pick-up. It will be sent to your registered email address!!

Your gift card containing your Credits Points will be valid for 2 years from its date of issue!
