    TRADE IN FAQs

    When you trade-in with us, the equivalent cash value of your trade-in will be given to you in Credits Points via an Email. Credits Points can be spent on any books in our online store, or physical stores (if any). Do take note Credits Points cannot be exchanged for cash.

    Credits Points you earn from trading-in with us can be spent across multiple orders/transactions. They will expire 2 years from the date of issue.

    QUICK TIP: As you don't have to spend your credit points in a single transaction, we recommend saving / starring our email(s) so you can quickly access your credit points in future!

    If you can't find our email(s) / your credit points, drop us an email at support@mentari!

    We use an in-house algorithm (based on current market price, popularity, market saturation and condition grade) to determine each book's trade-in value. At present, we do not offer pre-valuation.

    Look out for an email* or two from us, containing your credit points and a breakdown of the value of your books, 3 or 5 days after we receive your books.

    You may receive your credit points across different emails, as due to the nature of our operations, different book genre are processed separately.

    *Do check your spam/junk if you can't find our email(s)!

    Of course! If you choose to donate your books, instead of trading them in for credit points, we will in turn donate your credit point as cash to nonprofit organisations and initiatives to support efforts in tackling urgent environmental or social issues, when they are sold.
